Unfold Movement Studio believes in offering a welcoming space for all fitness levels, backgrounds and desired wellness goals. Our studio is built on providing a tranquil escape from the busyness of day to day life, and a safe space to start your own unique journey towards reaching your fullest wellness and fitness potential. We are here to challenge the body and encourage the mind that we may become the most empowered versions of ourselves. And we look forward to taking this transformative journey with you.

Our Practice:
We follow the original Pilates principles of Alignment, Control, Precision, Focus, Balance, Core power and Functional strength, as well as incorporating a variety of fitness elements, including Aerial and Yoga practices, to create a holistic and dynamic approach to our classes, designed to recalibrate, rebalance and renew the body and mind, to function within its most efficient and effective capacity, with direction, thought, and purpose.

About Marisa:
With a long held passion for fitness, self-development and awareness, and expression through movement, I come from an extensive background in Modern, Contemporary, and African Dance styles, with a BA in Dance from the University of Cape Town, a Pilates Instructor Qualification from the Exercise Teachers Academy of Cape Town, received with Distinction, and an Aerial Yoga Teachers Qualification from The Yoga Mala certified by YACEP.

Class Info:
We offer group and private Pilates Mat and Aerial classes of 1 hour per session. Please contact Marisa for private class time-slot availability.

Group classes are limited to 6 clients per session, thus booking is essential and we hold to a 24 hour cancellation policy or the class payment may be forfeit. For missed classes which have been paid for and cancelled by the client in advance, the class credit can be used for a make-up class, or given to a friend to be used within the same month. We operate on a discounted monthly fee structure or a weekly Drop-in class rate.

IMPORTANT:
Your monthly payment at the start of each calendar month reserves your spot in your desired classes for that month. If you work on the weekly payment Drop-in rate for occasional or irregular attendance, you will need to check class space availability before each class you wish to attend.
