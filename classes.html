Class Info:

We offer group and private Pilates Mat and Aerial classes of 1 hour per session. Please contact Marisa for private class time-slot availability.

Group classes are limited to 6 clients per session, thus booking is essential and we hold to a 24 hour cancellation policy or the class payment may be forfeit. For missed classes which have been paid for and cancelled by the client in advance, the class credit can be used for a make-up class, or given to a friend to be used within the same month. We operate on a discounted monthly fee structure or a weekly Drop-in class rate.

Important: Your monthly payment at the start of each calendar month reserves your spot in your desired classes for that month. If you work on the weekly payment Drop-in rate for occasional or irregular attendance, you will need to check class space availability before each class you wish to attend.

A bespoke Pilates and Aerial Fitness home Studio run by Marisa Smuts in tranquil Constantia, Cape Town.
Offering group & private classes for all fitness levels and goals.
